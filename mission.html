Our Vision

Our Mission

To focus on creating an excellence-driven, comprehensive, compassionate, free of charge and replicable healthcare system accessible to all. To be a not-for-profit entity, managed with the Islamic concept of Waqf and funded through donations and Zakat. To adhere to ethical best practices in all aspects of its operations, while following the Shariah and the Law of the Land. To empower its employees for their spiritual and professional growth To enhance and build human capacities through quality education and research.
