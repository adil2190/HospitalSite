Services

Clinical Services

We offer a range of clinical services at the various campuses across Pakistan. Visit the hospital sites to view complete list of services offered.

Blood Center

We are Pakistan’s first standardized blood center servicing the patient needs within the Indus Health Network and other philanthropic hospitals in Pakistan.

Physical Rehabilitation Center

The Physical Rehabilitation Center with the support of International Committee of the Red Cross and CHAL Foundation was established in 2015 and has since provided 600 prosthetic limbs and 2000 orthotic devices.
